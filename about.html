About Me

My Journey

With over 17 years of experience in the field of data science and artificial intelligence, I've dedicated my career to developing innovative solutions that transform raw data into actionable business insights.

I hold an M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence from Brunel University London, where I specialized in machine learning algorithms and their practical applications in business contexts.

Professional Experience

Throughout my career, I've led cross-functional teams to design and implement AI solutions that drive operational efficiency and business growth. My expertise spans the entire AI development lifecycle, from data collection and preprocessing to model deployment and monitoring.

I've worked across various industries, including retail, finance, healthcare, and manufacturing, giving me a broad perspective on how AI can be applied to solve diverse business challenges.

My Approach

I believe in a pragmatic approach to AI development, focusing on solutions that deliver real business value. I'm particularly passionate about the intersection of traditional machine learning and generative AI, where I've developed several innovative applications that push the boundaries of what's possible with current technology.

My philosophy is that the best AI solutions are those that augment human capabilities rather than replace them, creating a synergy between human expertise and machine intelligence.

Beyond Work

When I'm not coding or designing AI systems, I enjoy mentoring aspiring data scientists and contributing to open-source projects. I'm also an avid reader of science fiction and enjoy exploring how speculative fiction can inspire real-world technological innovations.
