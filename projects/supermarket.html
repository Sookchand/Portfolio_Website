Project Overview

I developed a comprehensive AI-powered platform that integrates multiple data sources to provide a holistic view of the supply chain and enable data-driven decision-making across multiple business functions.

The system combines traditional Machine Learning with Generative AI to analyze data from three key sources:

  • Supermarket Sales Data: Transaction-level data capturing customer purchases, product details, and sales metrics
  • Retail and Warehouse Sales Data: Channel-specific sales data across different product categories and suppliers
  • Marketing and Product Performance Data: Campaign metrics, customer engagement, and product performance indicators

By integrating these diverse datasets, the system provides actionable insights for inventory management, marketing optimization, pricing strategies, and supply chain operations. Users can interact with the system through a natural language interface, asking complex questions and receiving detailed analyses, visualizations, and recommendations.

Key Features

Enhanced AI Agent Architecture

The system employs a modular architecture with specialized AI agents that work together to provide comprehensive insights and recommendations:

Explanation Agent

Translates complex data insights into natural language explanations that are easy to understand for business users.

Recommendation Agent

Generates actionable business recommendations based on data analysis and best practices across multiple business functions.

Simulation Agent

Simulates different business scenarios to predict outcomes and help with decision-making, incorporating data from all integrated sources.

Orchestration Agent

Coordinates the specialized agents and provides a unified interface for user interaction across the integrated platform.

Inventory Agent

Specializes in inventory optimization, providing insights and recommendations for optimal inventory levels and allocation across channels.

Marketing Agent

Focuses on marketing campaign optimization, customer segmentation, and ROI analysis using integrated marketing performance data.

Pricing Agent

Specializes in price optimization and elasticity modeling, recommending optimal pricing strategies for different products and channels.

Enhanced Visualization Capabilities

The system generates a comprehensive range of visualizations that leverage the integrated datasets to provide deeper insights:

Customer Segment Analysis

Visualizes customer segments based on purchasing behavior, demographics, and cross-channel engagement.

Price Optimization

Shows the impact of price changes on sales volume, revenue, and profitability across different channels.

Marketing Campaign ROI

Visualizes the return on investment for different marketing strategies and customer segments.

Customer Satisfaction Analysis

Identifies factors affecting customer ratings and suggests improvements.

Inventory Optimization

Visualizes optimal inventory levels and allocation across retail and warehouse channels.

Channel Performance

Compares performance metrics across retail and warehouse sales channels.

Enhanced Streamlit Interface

The system features a responsive and intuitive web interface built with Streamlit, providing interactive visualizations, natural language query capabilities, and customizable dashboards that integrate data from all sources.

The natural language query interface allows users to ask complex questions about the integrated data
